About Us

Welcome to the Heron Party.

The Empire of Great Britain needs sensibility and stability more than ever. Following term after term of continuous upending, it's time for us to focus on securing what we have.

Through the principles of Liberal Conservatism, the Heron Party believes in the independence of individuals on economic matters, allowing them to self-govern and preserve. However, we also understand the need for state presence in law, order, and welfare.

It is the responsibility of the government to facilitate an adequate standard of living, but acknowledge that everyone deserves happiness and recognition equally, regardless of background or belief.

Therefore, the Heron Party stands for the presence of state healthcare and benefits, but the encouragement of economic mobility and independence for all individuals.

The path of security will see success, and our success will come from our sensibility.

Vote security. Vote success. Vote Heron.
